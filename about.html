Welcome to Artravel!


Artravel is a travel blog dedicated to contemporary art-related travels.

Fellow travelers and art lovers can come here for posts about destinations and spots to enjoy contemporary art.

We offer guides on how to plan the perfect art trip, shares stories, and recommendations.

We are a growing community and look forward to engage with other artists and art professionals to create a supportive group or art lovers around the world.




About Monalisa

Artravel Editor

Hello travelers and fellow art lovers!

My name is Monalisa and I am here to share my stories and my art trips around the world.

I am a contemporary art curator and passionate solo traveler, always on the hunt for new destinations, museums, and galleries.

I was only a few months old when my parents brought me on my first trip and ever since then traveling has been my biggest passion. Over the years contemporary art has come into play as my other big passion. It is now a huge part of my life, both for my career and when I am traveling. I strongly believe it is the perfect medium to discover new places, get to know different points of view, and dive into new cultures.

I decided to create this blog to share my stories, remember my trips, and possibly help you and inspire you in your Artravel. Feel free to reach out if you have any suggestion, if you want to discuss any destination or if you just want to talk about your trips!
